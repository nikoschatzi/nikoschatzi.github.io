Accelerated Life Tests

An effective method for evaluating the degradation and aging of electronics is through accelerated life testing (ALT). This testing simulates the long-term effects of environmental factors, such as temperature, on the performance and reliability of electronic components.

To evaluate the performance of Analog-to-Digital Converters (ADCs), custom PCBs were designed with integrated IC sockets, allowing for easy swapping and testing of various components.
