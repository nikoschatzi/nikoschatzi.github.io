MEMBERSHIP

A structured path
to the network

Vertex operates on a tier system. Entry requires a completed application reviewed manually by VertExperts. Advancement is earned through contribution to the network.

TIER 01

Member

Application-based entry

Access to the Vertex network. Peer discussions, event access, and the open knowledge base.

  • Network access & directory
  • Community discussions
  • Public knowledge articles
  • Monthly digest newsletter

TIER 02 — SENIOR

Senior Member

By nomination or track record

Priority project matching, mentoring sessions, and full access to the premium knowledge vault.

  • All Member benefits
  • Priority project flow
  • VertExpert mentoring sessions
  • Premium knowledge vault
  • Private event access

TIER 03

VertExpert

By invitation from founding circle

The inner circle. VertExperts shape the network, conduct vetting, and earn from consulting and mentoring.

  • All Senior benefits
  • Vetting & screening rights
  • Consulting revenue share
  • Mentoring revenue (85%)
  • Founding circle access

Apply for membership

Applications are reviewed manually. We assess credentials, track record, and technical depth. Not all applicants are accepted.

Review takes 5–10 business days. All applications are confidential.

WHAT WE LOOK FOR

Verifiable expertise

A clear and documented track record. Major projects, institutions, or research that can be independently verified.

Technical depth

Surface-level generalism is not sufficient. We look for engineers who have operated at the boundary of their discipline.

Contribution intent

Vertex is only valuable if members contribute. We prioritise applicants who bring knowledge and are willing to share it.

Professional conduct

The network relies on mutual respect and discretion. References or endorsements from current members significantly strengthen an application.
